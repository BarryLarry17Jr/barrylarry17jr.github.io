The Life of Barry

Barry was procured as a young 'wipa snippa' at a very young age.
Barry also likes eating different types of panty cakes.

Below there will be a compilation of different types of beef wellingtons.


Now that you have had a gander at the beef wellingtons, you will now go on a short adventure to find Barry!


The instructions are as follows:

  1. Click the beef wellington below.
  2. Once directed to an array of beef wellingtons, chose one of them wisely.
  3. If you chose incorrectly you will be directed to the beginning. Alternatively if you choose correctly, you will be administered to even more beef wellingtons!
  4. Barry will enlighten you once all the correct beef wellingtons have been chosen. And don't forget if you choose the wrong beef wellington you will have to restart.
  5. Have fun playing!
