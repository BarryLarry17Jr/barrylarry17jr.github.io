Scissor Paper Rock

Scissor-Paper-Rock is a hand game usually played between two people.
Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand.
These shapes are "rock", "paper", and "scissors".
However this version is played digitaly.

The rules are simple:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Paper beats rock
  • Scissors beats paper
